Features

Data Collection

Gathering diverse and rich datasets from IMDb, social media platforms, and drama review websites, our app meticulously analyzes viewer ratings, social media mentions, and detailed cast and crew information to create a comprehensive data foundation for prediction.

Data Pre-processing

Before predictions begin, data is cleaned and preprocessed to ensure quality. Techniques such as normalization and one-hot encoding of categorical variables are applied to prepare the data for efficient model training, directly impacting predictive performance.

Model Training

Utilizing advanced algorithms like Linear Regression, Logistic Regression, and SVM, the app trains on historical data to identify patterns. These patterns are then used to predict the success of new Broadway shows with a high degree of accuracy.
